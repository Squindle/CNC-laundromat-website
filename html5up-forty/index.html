About Us

Here at Clean and Cotch we believe that efficiency and comfort are of paramount importance and strive to bring that to you each day. Inside, you will not only find a massive selection of washers and driers available to book and rent, but you will also find a cosy cafe with lots of snacks and refreshments for you to enjoy while waiting for your washing. We are also committed to giving our loyal patrons exciting experiences and shows at our indoor stage. Here we will display a variety of talent from DJ Sets to open mics and everything in between to give back to our customers.
